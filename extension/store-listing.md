## Detailed description

**Is that recruiter real?**

Fake-job scams cost people billions every year. ANVIX is a one-click
investigator that tells you if a message on LinkedIn, Gmail, WhatsApp Web,
or Outlook is likely a scam — before you reply, click a link, or send
money.

**How it works**
1. Highlight the suspicious message (recruiter DM, offer email, WhatsApp
   text).
2. Right-click → **Investigate with ANVIX**.
3. ANVIX opens with the message pre-filled and instantly runs:
   • ML-based scam scoring trained on 15k+ real scam offers
   • Live domain, SSL, DNS, SPF, DMARC checks
   • Community-signal match (has this email/phone/domain been reported before?)
   • A plain-English verdict with confidence band ("42 ± 8, likely scam")
   • Downloadable PDF report and shareable public link

**What it does NOT do**
• No background page reading
• No browsing history tracking
• No ads, no trackers
• The extension only activates when you right-click

**Privacy**
Community signals are SHA-256-hashed with a pepper before they leave your
browser — the raw email, phone, or handle never touches our database.
Full policy: https://anvix-trust-scan.lovable.app/privacy

**Open**
The web app is free to use. Source and roadmap live in the ANVIX repo.
